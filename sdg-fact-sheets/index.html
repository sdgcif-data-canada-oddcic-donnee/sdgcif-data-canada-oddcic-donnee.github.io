SDG Fact Sheets

Oct 20, 2020
  • SDG
  • Infographics
STC SDG Team

On 1 January 2016, the world officially began implementation of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development - the transformative plan of action based on 17 Sustainable Development Goals - to address urgent global challenges over the next 15 years. This series of fact sheets presents an overview of the 17 Goals using data currently available to report on the SDGs.
